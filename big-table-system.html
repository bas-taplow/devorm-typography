Introduction

Big Table can be configured to the exact needs of your space. Thanks to its smart modular frame, the table takes various heights and expands up to 10 metres in length with a minimum amount of legs. Available in desk, counter, and bar heights, with aluminium, steel, and wooden leg options. Choose from a wide selection of tabletop finishes and shapes to create the appearance your interior requires. A fully customisable table system for any working, meeting, or dining setting.

A table for every space

From a compact meeting room desk to a 10-metre shared workspace, Big Table handles the full range. Three heights, eight shapes, and a broad palette of finishes make it suitable for offices, hospitality, and dining settings alike.
