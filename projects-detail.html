Inspiration • OZMA PR

OZMA PR

OZMA PR's corporate blue shapes a compact Tokyo office
Office Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo, Japan
by SIGNAL Inc.

Designed by SIGNAL Inc., this renovation for PR agency OZMA PR transforms a long-cherished office with low ceilings and a compact layout into a workspace with its own visual identity. Rather than treating these spatial characteristics as limitations, the design works with them. OZMA PR's corporate blue runs throughout the interior, tying the rooms together. The concept, 'Coloured Living', approaches the office as a place to live rather than a conventional workspace, one that supports both comfort and concentration in daily working life.

Adaptable seating for changing needs

Making the most of a compact floor plan

Low ceilings and a tight layout are treated as given conditions, not problems to solve. The consistent material palette and well-proportioned furniture give each room a clear function without requiring more space.
